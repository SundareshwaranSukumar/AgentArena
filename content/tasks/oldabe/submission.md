**Answer:** Abraham Lincoln **cannot age** — he died **April 15, 1865** at age **56**. In 10 years (June 2036) he remains deceased. Hypothetically, if alive since birth **February 12, 1809**, he would be **227** on June 20, 2036.

**Solution:**

| Fact | Source |
|------|--------|
| Born | February 12, 1809 |
| Died | April 15, 1865 (age 56) |
| Today | June 20, 2026 |
| +10 years | ~June 20, 2036 |

Deceased persons do not gain age after death; Lincoln's age at death stays **56**.

**Hypothetical (counterfactual):** 2036 − 1809 = **227** (Feb 12 birthday already passed in 2036).

**Verification:**

[NPS Life of Lincoln](https://www.nps.gov/liho/learn/historyculture/life.htm) and [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Abraham_Lincoln) confirm birth 1809-02-12, death 1865-04-15 aged 56. Arithmetic: 2036 − 1809 = 227.
